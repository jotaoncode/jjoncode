Rotate Translate and Scale in Blender

Objectives

In the following video we can see how to make the basic transformations of blender for creating new designs in 3D printing.

Conclusion

Commands to produce a transformation:

Press S to scale

Press G to translate

Press R to rotate

At continuation of any of this transformations you can either press the Axis you want to transform, or exclude one of the axis as shown in the video
