How does it look like to paint PLA with acrylic?

Objectives

3D print a model from Thingiverse, and try to paint it with acrylic

Conclusion

It is important to make the removal of bad parts that were not 3D Printed properly, by removing plastic that is not well done.

Consider adding support to avoid problems in the print.

The better definition of the model the best also in regards to the spread of the paint. Most likely that the paint will spread across the lines of the model, a bit more dry paint was needed.

This is printed with a Creality CR 10, PLA, at a temperature of: 190 Nooze temperature, 50 for the bed.
