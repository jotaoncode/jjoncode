Space War

Objectives

To learn about Phaserjs by creating my very first and short game. This is only for desktop.

The art

Well, everything of this game is mine, in exception with the explosion and the laser fire that I took from another game in the examples of phaser js.

Game Instructions

You can move the spaceship with the cursors, and you can fire with the space bar. It is very similar to galaxian but only with 4 enemies XD. If any of this aliens touchs you, you loose. Have fun!

Conclusion

In this case we see a bit more about frameworks. Backbone Marionette and CSS. We worked with Layouts, Collections, Collection Views, Item Views, and CSS3 transitions.
